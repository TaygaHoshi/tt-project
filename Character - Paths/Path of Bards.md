## Medium Base Health
Your base health is 70.

## Echo
Choose a creature within 3 meters at the start of your turn. This creature can take one action this turn instead of you. 

You are still able to use your minor action normally.

## Magical Poetry
You can upkeep your magical songs and poems to affect allies or foes. Songs and poems can be started with a *speak* special action used with an action or a rapid action and you can keep them up freely as long as you keep speaking each round.

You can only have one song or poem active at any time. If your target gets out of range or you become unable to sing in any way, your upkeep ends. You can also end your song or poem freely at any time.

## In Rhythm
By spending 2 energy during your turns, you can use both a rapid action and a prepared action. 

## Resonance
When you start a new song or poem, you can choose to apply regeneration for 2 rounds. 

## Duet
An ally assists you with your singing. When you start singing a new song or a poem, an ally who can hear you can pay half the energy cost of that technique.

## Fade Out
By spending 1 energy at the start of your turn, you can use the *speak* special action for upkeeping your singing freely without needing an action or a rapid action. Using this ability ends your upkeep next turn.

# Techniques
## Song of Beauty
**Category:** Utility
**Execution Time:** Special
**Cost:** 4 energy
**Duration:** Up to 1 hour
**Effect**
	Subjects of your song are perceived better-looking in the eyes of others. Allies gain a +1 bonus to their social skill rolls and meeting rolls. 

## Song of Travel
**Category:** Utility
**Execution Time:** Special
**Cost:** 6 energy
**Duration:** Up to 1 hour
**Effect**
	Allies hearing your song gain a +10 kilometers of bonus to their daily travel speed.

## Poem of Fury
**Category:** Offense
**Execution Time:** Special
**Cost:** 4 energy
**Duration:** Up to 4 rounds
**Effect**
	Your poem induces might and courage within allies, increasing their potencies by 2. 

## Poem of Freedom
**Category:** Defense
**Execution Time:** Special
**Cost:** 4 energy
**Duration:** Up to 2 rounds
**Effect**
	Allies hearing you are unaffected by immobilized and slowed status effects.
