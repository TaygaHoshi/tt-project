# Armor
This equipment protects its wearer from physical and magical damage, depending on its type. See the [[Combat#Damage#Armor|Armor section in Combat.md]].

# Armor Types and Armor Rating

```armor_rating_table
Light      |       5        |       0       |  -  |    -    |
Adventurer |       5        |       5       | -1  |   -2    |
Heavy      |      10        |       0       | -2  |   -5    |
```
