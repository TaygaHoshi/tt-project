# Techniques
## Heal Area
**Category:** Defense
**Execution Time:** Action 
**Cost:** 4 energy
**Radius:** (2 + C/2) meters
**Effect**
	Heal all allies within radius for (10 + P). 

## Protect Ally
**Category:** Defense
**Execution Time:** Action
**Cost:** 2 energy
**Range:** 5 meters
**Duration:** (1 + C/2) rounds
**Effect**
	Choose an ally, heal them for (5 + P/2) and apply protected for the duration.
