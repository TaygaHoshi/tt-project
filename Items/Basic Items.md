# Currency
While trading is still very common, iron coins are the basic universally accepted currency. 

When large quantities of currency is required, gold coins are sometimes used for simplification. One gold coin is worth one thousand iron coins. 

# Containers
## Belt Pouch
**Category:** Container
**Base Price:** 150 iron coins
**Bulk:** Low capacity container, medium size
**Description:** 
	A relatively small bag that can be worn sideways.
	When worn, it grants one quick access slot for a small item. 
**Crafting:**
	Requires leatherworking equipment. 
	Success threshold: 10 husbandry.
	Duration: One day of work. 
	Materials: 1 unit of worked leather, 1 unit of felt and 1 unit of common metals.

## Shoulder Bag
**Category:** Container
**Base Price:** 50 iron coins
**Bulk:** Low capacity container, large size
**Description:** 
	A very small bag on a long stick. It is most useful for carrying rations. 
	This container can't be equipped during combat. 
**Crafting:**
	Requires leatherworking equipment. 
	Success threshold: 7 husbandry.
	Duration: Half a day of work. 
	Materials: 1 unit of worked leather. 

## Quiver
**Category:** Container
**Base Price:** 100 iron coins
**Bulk:** Ammo container, large size
**Description:** 
	A long bag that can be worn sideways or to the back.
	When worn, it can hold up to 30 arrows or 6 throwing spears.
